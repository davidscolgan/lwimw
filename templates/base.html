About LWIMW

Welcome to “Look What I Made Weekend” – where creative and motivated people make something and share the results with each other.

It’s pretty simple: you have approximately 48 hours to “make something” over the course of a weekend. To make it time-effective, we recommend you make something small and manageable – i.e. don’t create an entire art exhibit – make one piece instead.

What can you make? Pretty much whatever you want – a video game, painting, short film, short screenplay, drawing, sculpture, poem. You can start a new project or continue an existing one as long as you finish some version of it (it won’t be perfect – that’s not the point) by the end of the weekend.

Using this blog, you can post (or share a link or a photo) of what you made, and we all get to enjoy the awesomeness of your creative output!
